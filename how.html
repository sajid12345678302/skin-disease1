How it Works

  • Image Upload: Users upload a skin image for analysis.
  • Preprocessing: The image is resized to 128x128 and converted to grayscale for model input.
  • Model: The CNN has 3 convolutional layers followed by dense layers for classification.
  • Prediction: The model predicts the likely disease and confidence.
  • Built With: Python, Flask, TensorFlow, Keras.
